What Is Bitcoin Networks?

1
Bitcoin Networks is a Token

Bitcoin Networks (BTCN) is a minable token deployed on the only compatible blockchain: Binance Smart Chain. Bitcoin Networks utilizes BEP20 architecture to achieve its functionality.

2
Everyone can Mine!

Bitcoin Networks allows all of its users to mine it through a fair system for everyone! The mining formula is simple and easy to understand: Tokens Owned By You/Circulating Supply*Block Reward.

3
Halving Intervals

Just like any other mineable coin, Bitcoin Networks has mining intervals. After each halving interval, the block reward and time halves, whereas the total phase time doubles..

How It Works

BEP-20(BSC)

Bitcoin Networks is based on the BEP-20 interface and deployed upon Binance Smart Chain, which is fast, secure, and has low transaction costs

Mineable

Bitcoin Networks is the first-ever token that everyone can mined and has a fair mining system, which disallows the concentration of tokens in just a few hands.

Halving

Bitcoin Networks (BTCN) has four halving intervals, starting from 200 and ending at 25, making it possible to complete the mining in 13 years.
